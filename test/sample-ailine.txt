“Which commit produced this model?”

If your honest answer is often “probably main, plus some local changes,” you do not have lineage—you have hope.

AIline is an ML experiment tracker that snapshots the *actual* worktree (including uncommitted state), ties it to DVC-managed data and MLflow runs, and keeps a restore-friendly record of what executed.

One command pattern: `ailine track -- your training command`

Reproducibility is a workflow, not a slogan.
