SlopFilter Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
Applies to: SlopFilter and SlopFilter (Beta) browser extensions

Summary

SlopFilter runs entirely in your browser. We do not operate servers that receive your browsing data, posts, or classification results.

What the extension accesses

  • Page content on Reddit and LinkedIn — post text is read from the page DOM so it can be scored locally. Text is not sent to our servers.
  • Extension storage — your settings are stored with Chrome sync if enabled.
  • Local statistics — scan/flag counts in the popup are kept on your device.

What leaves your device

On first use, the extension may download the open-source TMR AI text detector model from Hugging Face (onnx-community/tmr-ai-text-detector-ONNX). Files are cached by the browser. No API keys required.

What we do not collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No accounts
  • No sale of personal data
  • No cloud classification API

Third parties

  • Hugging Face — model hosting
  • Google Chrome Web Store — distribution

Children

Not directed at children under 13.
